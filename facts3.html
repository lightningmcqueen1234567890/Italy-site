Italy has Europes only active volcanos! Mt. Etna, Mt. Stromboli, and Vesuvius! While
Etna and Stromboli erupt on a regular basis, Vesuvius' last eruption was way back in 1944!

(Above) Locals watch as Vesuvius erupts in 1944, right in the middle of an American raid aimed
at kicking the axis out of Italy. Many of the B-22 Bombers got ariel pictures of the eruption!

(Above) Just another day for the locals! Despite Mt. Etna erupting on a regular basis, cities and
towns cram themselves at its base, as the volcanic soil is some of the most fertile on earth. That's
why the Italian island of Sicily is so famous for its agriculture!

(Above) Stromboli is a tiny island off the coast of Italy, yet it is easily the most ferocious of the three!
There is however a small town on the tiny island! Those townsfolk must have a hard time walking with those balls of steel.
